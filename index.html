Hi, I'm Brendan.

I live between Boston and Shanghai. I am a member of the Abenaki Nation of Missisquoi. I speak both English and Chinese. I enjoy music, traveling, dimsum, geocaching and hiking with my family.

I currently work for Berkshire Hathaway on their Berxi platform. Previously, I worked in a number of education technology roles at EF (Education First).

In 2023 I founded Native Futures, an organization dedicated to leveraging emerging technologies to benefit Native American people. Our first initiative is Pathway, a generative AI chatbot designed to support underserved Native American students in preparing for college or university. This is presently in closed alpha.
